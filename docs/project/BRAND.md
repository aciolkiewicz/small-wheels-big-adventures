# Brand

## Purpose

This document defines the identity, personality and communication principles of the **Małe Koła, Wielkie Przygody** brand.

It ensures consistency across the website, mobile application, printable guides and all future communication channels.

---

# Brand Promise

> Helping families spend more time together and less time planning.

Everything we create should support this promise.

---

# Mission

We inspire families to explore nearby places through carefully curated, practical and trustworthy recommendations.

---

# Vision

To become Poland's most trusted family travel brand for one-day adventures.

---

# Brand Personality

If the brand were a person, it would be:

- Calm
- Friendly
- Knowledgeable
- Helpful
- Practical
- Curious
- Trustworthy

The brand never feels:

- Arrogant
- Sales-driven
- Overly enthusiastic
- Clickbait-oriented

---

# Brand Archetypes

Primary:

- Explorer

Secondary:

- Caregiver

The Explorer inspires discovery.

The Caregiver ensures every recommendation feels safe and well prepared.

---

# Voice

The voice should always be:

- clear
- warm
- practical
- honest
- encouraging

Avoid unnecessary marketing language.

---

# Tone

Our tone changes depending on the context.

Planning a route:

- calm
- informative

Storytelling:

- inspiring
- emotional

Safety information:

- direct
- precise

---

# Writing Style

Prefer practical advice over promotional descriptions.

Instead of:

> Amazing place!

Write:

> There is a large shaded playground located next to the picnic area.

---

# Editorial Principles

We only recommend places we would confidently recommend to our own families.

If a recommendation cannot be trusted, it should not be published.

---

# Brand Values

- Family
- Trust
- Nature
- Simplicity
- Quality
- Curiosity

---

# Logo Philosophy

The visual identity should communicate:

- movement
- exploration
- family
- nature
- simplicity

Minimalism is preferred over excessive decoration.

---

# Photography Style

Photography should feel authentic.

Prefer:

- natural light
- real families
- real locations
- candid moments

Avoid:

- stock photography
- excessive editing
- unrealistic staging

---

# Success

People should describe the brand as:

> "This guide feels like advice from a friend who has already been there."
