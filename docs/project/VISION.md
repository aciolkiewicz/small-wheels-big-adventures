# Vision

## Purpose

This document describes the long-term vision of the **Małe Koła, Wielkie Przygody** project.

Unlike the Project Charter, which explains why the project exists, this document describes what the project aims to become over the next several years.

---

# Our Vision

We believe that planning family adventures should be simple, enjoyable and inspiring.

Our goal is to build the most trusted platform for families looking for one-day trips with young children.

The platform should remove the stress of planning and replace it with excitement about spending meaningful time together.

---

# What We Are Building

We are not building a travel application.

We are building a **family travel ecosystem**.

Content is the foundation.

Every other product is simply another way of accessing that content.

---

# Long-term Goals

The project will evolve into a platform consisting of:

- Website
- Mobile application
- Printable travel guides
- Interactive maps
- GPX downloads
- Seasonal recommendations
- Editorial articles
- Community features

---

# Our Audience

The platform is designed primarily for families with children aged between 2 and 8 years.

Every feature should answer one question:

> "Will this make planning a family day easier?"

If the answer is no, the feature probably does not belong in the product.

---

# Our Definition of Quality

Quality is measured by usefulness.

Every published route should answer practical questions such as:

- Is it suitable for a bike seat?
- Is there enough shade?
- Where can we eat?
- Where is the nearest toilet?
- Where can the child play?
- Is public transport available?

The platform values practical information over promotional descriptions.

---

# Editorial Vision

We aim to become the most trusted editorial source for family trips.

Recommendations should be:

- honest
- practical
- carefully verified
- regularly updated

Editorial trust is more valuable than rapid growth.

---

# Product Vision

The platform should eventually provide:

- personalized trip recommendations
- offline access
- saved favourite routes
- completed trip history
- seasonal suggestions
- printable itineraries
- interactive planning tools

Technology is not the goal.

Technology exists only to improve the family's experience.

---

# Geographic Vision

The project starts in the Łódź region.

Once the editorial quality is proven, it may gradually expand to other regions of Poland.

Expansion should always follow quality.

Never the opposite.

---

# Community Vision

In the future, families may contribute:

- photographs
- trip reports
- corrections
- new attractions
- accessibility updates

However, all published content should remain editorially reviewed.

Trust is more important than quantity.

---

# Success

Success is achieved when families spend less time planning and more time creating memories together.

If planning a weekend trip takes less than five minutes, the platform fulfills its purpose.
