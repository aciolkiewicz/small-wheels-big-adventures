# Style Guide

## Purpose

This document complements the Writing Guidelines by providing practical examples of how content should be written.

Rules explain **what** to do.

Examples demonstrate **how** to do it.

---

# General Principle

Write as if you were recommending a trip to close friends with young children.

Be honest.

Be practical.

Be specific.

---

# Example 1 — Attraction

❌ Poor

The park is beautiful and definitely worth visiting.

✅ Better

The park offers wide paved paths suitable for strollers and bike trailers. The playground is located close to the main entrance, making it an easy first stop for younger children.

---

# Example 2 — Restaurant

❌ Poor

A great restaurant with delicious food.

✅ Better

The restaurant provides high chairs, a children's menu and a covered outdoor seating area. Service is usually fast, which is helpful when visiting with younger children.

---

# Example 3 — Playground

❌ Poor

Large playground.

✅ Better

The playground is fenced and divided into areas for toddlers and older children. Several benches are placed in the shade, allowing parents to comfortably supervise their children.

---

# Example 4 — Route Introduction

❌ Poor

This is one of the best cycling routes in the region.

✅ Better

This route combines quiet forest roads with several family-friendly stops, making it suitable for children travelling in bike seats or trailers.

---

# Example 5 — Honest Recommendation

❌ Poor

Perfect for everyone.

✅ Better

The route is suitable for most families, although the final climb may require pushing the bike for approximately 200 metres.

---

# Example 6 — Practical Information

❌ Poor

Parking is available.

✅ Better

The main car park is free and located approximately 150 metres from the route's starting point.

---

# Example 7 — Child Perspective

❌ Poor

There is a pond.

✅ Better

Many children enjoy feeding ducks from the wooden platform located next to the pond. Consider bringing appropriate bird food if permitted.

---

# Example 8 — Seasonal Advice

❌ Poor

Best visited in spring.

✅ Better

Spring brings blooming orchards and comfortable temperatures. During summer weekends the area can become significantly busier.

---

# Example 9 — Accessibility

❌ Poor

Easy route.

✅ Better

The entire route follows paved or compact gravel paths with no stairs, making it suitable for bike trailers and strollers.

---

# Example 10 — Summary

❌ Poor

Highly recommended.

✅ Better

If you are looking for a relaxed half-day trip with plenty of opportunities for breaks, this route is an excellent choice for families with young children.

---

# Checklist

Before publishing, ask:

- Is the advice practical?
- Is every recommendation specific?
- Are there unnecessary adjectives?
- Does the text help parents make decisions?
- Would I trust this recommendation myself?
