# Editorial Guidelines

## Purpose

This document defines how content is created, reviewed and maintained.

---

# Editorial Mission

Create practical, trustworthy and inspiring guides that help families spend more time together outdoors.

---

# Editorial Values

## Accuracy

Information must be correct and verified.

---

## Practicality

Readers should immediately know:

- where to park
- where to eat
- where toilets are located
- where children can play
- whether bike trailers and child seats are suitable

---

## Honesty

We never exaggerate.

We never recommend places solely because they are popular.

---

## Empathy

Content should be written from the perspective of a parent planning a family day.

---

## Freshness

Content should be reviewed periodically.

Outdated information should be corrected quickly.

---

---

# Required Route Sections

Every published route should include:

- overview
- practical information
- route description
- attractions
- food & drinks
- playgrounds
- toilets
- accessibility
- family tips
- summary

---

# Never Publish

- unverified information
- promotional copy
- misleading titles
- outdated prices presented as guaranteed
- unsafe recommendations

---

# Editorial Rule

When in doubt:

Do not publish until verified.
